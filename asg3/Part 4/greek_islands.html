The island of Crete

Ancient ruins, clear blue seas, warm weather, sprawling beaches, breathtaking sunsets, lively nightlife, panaramic views, delicious cuisine, local seafood, friendly atmosphere, relaxed lifestyle -- yes the Greek Islands are a unique and memorable destination. Each island offers its own distinct slice of Greek culture and beauty, yet they are geographically nearby. So with all of the island hopping opportunities, a package is a great way to go -- we take on all of the hassles of the itinerary while you focus on the adventure!

All packages include round-trip airfare and 4-star accommodations (where available), and island hopping ferry transportation. Tours are optional but can be included upon request. Our prices are very competitive and our service is beyond compare. Contact us directly for more details.

Whatever type of island adventure you have in mind, we've probably got a package that fits your needs. But if not, our experienced and helpful staff can put a custom package together for you. We also have special group discount rates so be sure to inquire if your traveling party includes 10 or more.
